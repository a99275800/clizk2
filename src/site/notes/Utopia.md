## Utopia

----

<img style="float:left;max-width:300px;max-height:400px;border:1px dotted black;margin: 0px 10px 0px 0px;" src="files/_XG11WV46EMCTP6WBLJXY.jpg ">Thomas Moore published his book Utopia, a description of an Ideal State. Much later Tommaso Campanella (The City of the Sun), Francis Bacon (The New Atlantis) and Johannes Valentinus Andreae (Description of the Republic of Christianopolis) wrote comparable books. The books were based on Plato’s Republic. All of the books contained Architectual Designs based on Ancient Geometries.

Utopia, deriva do grego antigo 'ou', significando não, e 'topos', lugar [[S2006m).
