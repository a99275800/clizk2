## 70W66g o valor do desconforto conforme Georgia O'Keeffe e Popova

The Value of Being Uncomfortable: Herman Melville on Privation as a Portal to Appreciation and Aliveness. Maria Popova. Source: <https://www.brainpickings.org/2020/04/13/herman-melville-moby-dick-contrast/>

“**Anyone with any degree of mental toughness ought to be able to exist without the things they like most for a few months at least**” Georgia O’Keeffe,

only in the absence of our habitual comforts, without all the ways in which we ordinarily cushion against the hard facts of our own nature and our mortality, do we befriend ourselves and discover what is most alive in us. The contrast, uncomfortable at first, even painful, becomes a clarifying force. Without the superfluous, the essential is revealed.
