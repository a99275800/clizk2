## 37z13v propaganda não influencia realmente as pessoas

> no mesmo sentido: [[25w68j superestimamos o poder da midia de influenciar pessoas\|25w68j superestimamos o poder da midia de influenciar pessoas]] (marketing publicidade propaganda meios de comunicação)

> fonte: https://twitter.com/SteveStuWill/status/1161850271535788032

> Mercier, H. (2017). **How Gullible are We?** A Review of the Evidence from Psychology and Social Science. _Review of General Psychology_, _21_(2), 103-122. [https://doi.org/10.1037/gpr0000111](https://doi.org/10.1037/gpr0000111), acesso em 01 de novembro de 2023. 

**People are not particularly gullible, and not particularly susceptible to ads, political propaganda, or other attempts at persuasion**. [1/3]

Exhibit A: Advertising is much less effective than people tend to think. https://journals.sagepub.com/doi/abs/10.1037/gpr0000111  [2/3]

Exhibit B: The average effect of campaign contact during general elections is... zero. http://stanford.edu/~dbroock/published%20paper%20PDFs/kalla_broockman_minimal_persuasive_effects_of_campaign_contact_in_general_elections_evidence_from_49_field_experiments.pdf

It's really hard to change people's minds.
