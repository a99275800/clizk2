## 62Q21u teoria do humor ou por que nós rimos 

> cvç: isto está colidindo com o vb [[Humor\|Humor]]

The Benign Violation Theory of Humor​:​  No business school would ever teach a humor class, but being funny is more helpful than the majority of things you learn in an MBA.  Where does humor come from?  Here’s an answer from a book called Shtick to Business:  "A benign violation is a paradoxical situation in which something is wrong, threatening, or confusing (a violation), yet simultaneously okay, safe, or makes sense (benign). The resulting laughter says to the world, “That thing that seems wrong is actually okay.  The theory explains the two ways that humor attempts fail. If a situation is solely a violation, it produces outrage and disgust. If the situation is solely benign, it produces boredom.  To be funny, it takes a precise mix of both—the sweet spot in the center." (via Perell 1n 100620a)
