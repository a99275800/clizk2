## 89f45u respostas são influenciadas por eufemismos nas perguntas

People judge an action more favorably if it's described euphemistically, and less favorably if it's described dysphemistically (e.g., enhanced interrogation vs. torture; activist vs. extremist). The strategic use of such terms is not seen as dishonest. https://sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0010027721000524

Why is this surprising? Because the choice of words actually seemed to make a difference! A lot of ideas in psychology sound perfectly plausible but turn out not to be true.
