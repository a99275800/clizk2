To export notes highlights grifos grifadores anotações técnicas de estudo from a PDF file, I use a separate app called [Highlights](https://apps.apple.com/app/highlights-pdf-reader/id1498912833?ref=stories.ulysses.app). And to export notes from Apple Books, I use this workaround: I tap on the hamburger/outline icon, then on the Notes section, then on the share icon, choose “Edit Notes” → “Select All” → “Share” → “Mail”, then select all text, choose “Share” from a pop-up menu and only then save it to a selected app (see, Apple made it difficult, but not impossible)
