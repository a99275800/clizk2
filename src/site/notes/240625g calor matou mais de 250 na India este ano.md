Delhi hospital opens unit to treat victims of heatstroke. The Guardian 25 Jun 2024 Amrit Dhillon The hospital has seen well over 50 heatstroke patients in the past week. People in Delhi and much of north India have been battered by temperatures of 40 degrees or more every single day since midMay. March was the hottest month ever recorded. At the start of June, Delhi recorded its highest ever temperature, with two weather stations in the capital reporting 49C (120.2F) and 49.1C (120.38F). (...) According to newspapers that have collated figures, hospitals in Delhi have reported 275 deaths in the heatwave since mid-May. (...) The real figure is believed to be much higher.
