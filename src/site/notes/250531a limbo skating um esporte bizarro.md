Shristi Dharmendra Sharma, a gifted Indian skater, set a new world record in limbo skating by gliding under descending poles for 50 meters in an astonishing 9.59 seconds. This remarkable feat took place on December 18, 2023, during the television show *Lo Show Dei Record* in Monza, Italy!
Limbo skating is a demanding discipline that requires extraordinary flexibility, balance, and control. Athletes perform in a deep squat—often with legs in a full split—while maneuvering beneath extremely low obstacles.
Already a record-holder in this unique sport, Shristi continues to push boundaries and break her own records, inspiring skaters around the globe with her unmatched talent and dedication.
