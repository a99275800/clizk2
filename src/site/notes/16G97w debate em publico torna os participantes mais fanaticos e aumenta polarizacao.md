## 16G97w debate em público torna os participantes mais fanáticos e aumenta polarização 

> v também [[13m34k fake news falsas crencas por que nos apegamos vieses cognitivos radicalismo\|13m34k fake news falsas crencas por que nos apegamos vieses cognitivos radicalismo]]

> SCHKADE, David; SUNSTEIN, Cass R.; HASTIE, Reid. What Happened on Deliberation Day? SSRN Electronic Journal - California Law Review 915 (2007), 2007. Disponível em: <https://chicagounbound.uchicago.edu/cgi/viewcontent.cgi?article=12230&context=journal_articles>. Acesso em: 2020 May 5AD.

> Ev 050520c (radicalismo, polarização, fanatismo, psicologia)

O que aconteceu no dia do debate: (California Law Review) um experimento que reúne duas equipes de pontos de vista sabidamente diferentes para deliberarem juntas sobre assuntos polêmicos. O resultado: elas ficam mais radicalizadas.

"The major effect of deliberation was to make group members more extreme in their views than they were before they started to talk. Liberals became more liberal on all three issues; conservatives became more conservative. As a result of intragroup deliberation, the division between the citizens of Boulder and the citizens of Colorado Springs significantly increased. Deliberation also increased consensus and significantly reduced diversity within the groups. Even anonymous statements of personal opinion became more extreme and less diverse after deliberation"
