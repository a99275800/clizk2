## 59N79r conselhos de perell sobre escrever

1. Great writers comfort the confused and confuse the comforted. #narratologia  #arte #star
2. We tell stories when the truth is too painful to talk about directly.
3. Writing every day is the best way to become a better public speaker. Talking about things you’ve already written about it easy, and your ideas will be so well-structured that people will call you a genius.
4. Writing becomes a lot easier once you realize people only remember your best work.
5. Writing isn’t the byproduct of deep thinking. It's where deep thinking actually happens.
6. Writing shows you how little you know about topics you thought you were an expert on.
7. Create your own luck by writing online. Tell people what you’re working on, be open about your goals, and teach others what you learn.
8. If you can’t communicate an idea clearly, you haven’t written about it enough.
9. I’ve yet to meet an original thinker who doesn’t spend a lot of time alone.
10. Easy reading is hard writing.
