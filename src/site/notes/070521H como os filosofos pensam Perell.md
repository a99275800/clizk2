## 070521H como os filósofos pensam

IUI 070521H (dii) David Perell no Twitter: 

Understand ideas by making them battle with each other. Create characters in your mind and make them debate each other.

Think by writing

Writing is more than typing ( #sobre-escrever #arte)

**Ideas are like fashion. Since ideas are invisible, people underestimate the extent to which they can arbitrarily go in and out of style. We laugh at the things people in the 70s used to wear, but if we could see ideas in photos, we’d do the same for our thinking** (#star)

Copy-cat thinking is everywhere; People who don’t have the tools to reason independently make up their minds by adopting the opinions of prestigious people. When they do, **they favor socially rewarded positions over objective accounts of reality**

Beware of the Spotlight Effect (isto é, olhar para onde todo mundo está olhando)

Live with Intellectual Grace; “What can this person teach me?” is a much more productive question than “How is this person wrong?”

(Human nature doesn't change.)
