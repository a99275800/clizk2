## 25w68j superestimamos o poder da mídia de influenciar pessoas

> no mesmo sentido: [[37z13v propaganda nao influencia realmente pessoas\|37z13v propaganda nao influencia realmente pessoas]]. (propaganda publicidade marketing meios de comunicação)

> fonte: https://twitter.com/SteveStuWill/status/1067937098395570176

> Ferguson, C. J., Nielsen, R. K. L., & Markey, P. (2020). Movie smoking and teen smoking behavior: A critical methodological and meta-analytic review. _Psychology of Popular Media, 9_(2), 247–254. [https://doi.org/10.1037/ppm0000212](https://psycnet.apa.org/doi/10.1037/ppm0000212), acesso em 1 nov. 2023.

Other examples...

1. Media images have little effect on body dissatisfaction or eating disorders. Largest effect: genes. Second largest: peer influence. http://christopherjferguson.com/Who%20Is%20the%20Fairest.pdf

2. Media sex and violence has surprisingly little effect on real-world behaviour. http://looooker.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/11/jcom12129.pdf http://link.springer.com/article/10.1007%2Fs11126-016-9442-2

3. Do violent video games make people more violent? Nope - if anything, it's the other way around. https://houstonchronicle.com/local/gray-matters/article/dan-patrick-violent-video-games-santa-fe-shooting-12934193.php https://sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0091743517300579

Ban non-violent video games!

4. Do violent movies increase real-world violence? Nope – if anything, they do the reverse. https://interpersonalresearch.weebly.com/uploads/1/0/4/0/10405979/school-safety-written-testimony.pdf

Ban non-violent movies!

5. Does pornography increase the incidence of sexual aggression? Nope – once again, if anything, it has the opposite effect. http://christopherjferguson.com/pornography.pdf

See https://psychologytoday.com/intl/blog/all-about-sex/201601/evidence-mounts-more-porn-less-sexual-assault for a user-friendly overview of the evidence.

'On the other hand...'

Media coverage of mass shootings seems to increase the risk of future shootings. https://iza.org/publications/dp/11900/the-effect-of-media-coverage-on-mass-shootings
